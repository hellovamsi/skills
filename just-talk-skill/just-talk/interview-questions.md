# Interview Questions — Starter Prompts

Use these as rotating hint prompts at the start of each session. Pick 3 at random.

## Leadership & Strategy
- Tell me about a time you had to pivot a product strategy based on new data.
- Describe a time you had to align stakeholders with competing priorities.
- Tell me about a time you had to build or rebuild a product team.
- How have you mentored a junior product manager to become a senior leader?
- Describe a time you had to manage a project with significant ambiguity.

## Conflict & Problem Resolution
- Tell me about a time you had to say "no" to a product idea from a senior stakeholder.
- Describe a situation where you had to turn around a failing product or project.
- Tell me about a time you had a conflict with engineering or sales, and how you resolved it.
- Describe a time you made a high-stakes decision that failed. What did you learn?

## Execution & Impact
- Tell me about a time you used data to influence a major company decision.
- Describe a time you had to make a tough trade-off between quality and speed to market.
- Tell me about the most innovative product you launched and its business impact.
- Tell me about a time you had to manage a product portfolio rather than just one feature.
